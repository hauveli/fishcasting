      Focus Bobbers

      A truly remarkable invention for such a trite sport. A Focus Bobber not only extends my ambit by about an arms length from its origin, it also casts any stored hex within once I pull on it with enough force. This added range does not come for free however, as casting via the Focus Bobber costs Amethyst Dust equal to the square of the length of the vector from myself to the bobber.

      Fishing Patterns

      I came across a soggy book in my travels written by a sage fisher who went by the name 'Babs'. While most of the text consists of the ramblings of a sailor, found within the pages were some mundane patterns for fishing. While I don't see myself fishing one way or the other, I must note these down. These would come in handy I ever decide to use a Casting Rod.


      Bobbert's Purification (player → entity or null)

      • Renders
        • Palettes
          Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: weeed

          Transforms a player on the stack into the bobber attached to their fishing pole (or Null if there isn't one). Costs about one Amethyst Dust.


          Trebbob's Purification (entity → player)

          • Renders
            • Palettes
              Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: aqqqw

              Transforms a bobber on the stack into the player which cast it. Costs about one Amethyst Shard.


              Bob's Purification (entity → entity or null)

              • Renders
                • Palettes
                  Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: qaqqqw

                  Transforms an entity on the stack into the bobber it is caught on (or Null if there isn't one). Costs about one Charged Amethyst.


                  Babs' Purification (entity → entity or null)

                  • Renders
                    • Palettes
                      Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: weeede

                      Transforms a bobber on the stack into the entity caught on its hook (or Null if there isn't one). Costs about one Amethyst Shard.


                      Length Purification (entity → number)

                      • Renders
                        • Palettes
                          Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: wqaqw

                          Measure the length of the fish entity. Returns 0 if the entity could not be measured or was not a fish.
